About
Tommy Kneeland is a Boston-based funk and jazz horn player and a multi-instrumentalist who specializes in brass, playing every brass instrument there is. He's the lead trumpet player and primary instrumental arranger for Boston funk-pop band Hill House. He earned degrees in both computer science and music from WPI, and works a software day job while writing and recording funk by night.
